Implant prosthetics

Implant prosthetics deals with implant-supported prosthetic restoration. A dental implant is a surgical component that interfaces with the bone of the jaw or skull to support a dental prosthesis such as a crown, bridge, denture, facial prosthesis or to act as an orthodontic anchor. Prostheses can be created by hand or with computer-aided design, a software interface that helps creators design and analyze the creation with computer-generated 2-D and 3-D graphics as well as analysis and optimization tools.
